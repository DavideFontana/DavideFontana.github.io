MA IN PARADISO HANNO LE LUCI AL NEON?
3D
PROGETTO ACCADEMICO
2021_
Third millenium: Religions around the world are in a deep crisis. Faith, devotion and the practice of religious customs and traditions find less and less space in modern life. Belivers are gradually becoming extinct and with them the hope for an afterlife. The idea of a life of sacrifice and penance, away from sins, in the hope of reserving a spot in heaven, alongside one's God, is no longer aligned with individual priorities and principles. The image of a majestic and imposing staircase, lost in the clouds and radiated by celestial light, is no longer tenable.
