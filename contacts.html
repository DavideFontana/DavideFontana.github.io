ABOUT

Davide Fontana is an emerging artist studying at the "Accademia di Belle Arti di Brera", exploring a wide range of media including installation, video, animation, software and video games.
His practice focuses on the complexities of digital media and internet culture, seeking to reveal underlying structures and mechanisms that often remain obscured. Through a critical look at both technology and the society that uses it, he investigates the boundaries between digital and physical, fiction and reality, natural and artificial. His practice interrogates the fluid intersections of contemporary virtual landscapes. Davide's work has been exhibited at local and international exhibitions.
